WELCOME TO LITTLE ANGELS SCHOOL

Little angels the international School, your child’s second home has been Shaping Minds and Touching lives. The little angeles family started in 2002 with just one school in Hyderabad. Today, 19 years down the line, it has grown to include a chain of over 35 international schools that have impacted over 30,000 young lives. You can find little angeles school branches in most of the major cities in India, namely Bengaluru, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune, Kolkata, Chennai, and Gurugram. Ranked as one of the top CBSE International schools in the Times School Survey, we strive to set new benchmarks in the field of education.
